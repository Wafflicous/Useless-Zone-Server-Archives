pod_s 08/24/2020 7:21 PM
It's time to get your bling on, folks! Money is now generated every 20 or so seconds on the client [to prevent lag], and each gold bar is worth 3 wealth at the start [increased later on during research] https://gyazo.com/2cb3e01fe8e44ebd347598a4a4c861c9
19:25
Additionally, population has been added, and grows in a logarithmic format. To explain it without going too mathy, here's what it'll look like: -The population will seem to be growing exponentially, as your population grows you will receive more population, however.. -..there is a roof. This roof will be the point where population growth slows down, and eventually, grinds to a halt. Your population will grow very, very slowly once it hits this roof. This roof can be raised by researching certain things and expanding your land. -Your roof can also go below your current roof if a famine, disaster, etc.. occurs, causing a population decrease. This change was done to make population growth seem more realistic, as it follows real-life population growth formats found in nature and used by ecologists and people working in demographics.
